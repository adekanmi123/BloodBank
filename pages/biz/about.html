About Us

Our Goal

We want to be the largest portal in Kenya for blood donors. We want to make sure no one ever has to suffer or die because of a shortage of blood in the blood banks.

We shall do this by creating a database of potential donors. We shall enable people in need to send pleas using our portal. Our robust engine shall then do an analysis of donors based on blood group, location, date of last donation among other variables and send alerts to viable potential donors.
