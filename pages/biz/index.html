Hi There

According to a friend who works for a blood transfusion agency in Kenya, less than half of the blood needed for transfusions in Kenya is actually collected. Now I do not know about you but I think that paints a very grim picture for anyone who is in need of a blood transfusion. So what happens to those patients in the hospital? From experience, they seek out relatives and friends to donate (Its good to be popular :-)). If they do not have any family or friends? Your guess is as good as mine. What if you and I could change that? What if we could give life to everyone in hospital right now? How many lives can we save, together? 10? 50? 100? 1000? I believe the potential is endless. All we need to do is stand united and save lives.
